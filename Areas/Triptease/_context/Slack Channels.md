## Notes for Automations

- **`#global`** is the most-watched channel. Use sparingly — only company-wide-relevant posts.
- **`#ai-chat`** is the right place for AI tool launches, MCP demos, skill announcements.
- **`#mktg-banter`** and `#team-marketing` are appropriate for Niko's project announcements.
- **`#dbs-*` channels** are short-lived and event-specific.
- **Squad channels** are the right place for product-specific automation outputs (e.g. Parity insights → `#squad-rockhopper`).
- **BirthdayBot, CelebrationBot** are existing automations — don't duplicate their function.
