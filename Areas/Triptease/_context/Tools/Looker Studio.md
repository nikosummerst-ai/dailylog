# Looker Studio

## What It Does
Google's dashboard and reporting tool (formerly Google Data Studio) for client-facing and internal reporting.

## How We Use It
- CS, Sales, and GTM create client-facing and internal reporting dashboards
- Campaign performance reports shared directly with hotel clients
- Internal GTM reporting and analysis

## Access
- Dashboard: https://lookerstudio.google.com

## Used By
- CS — client-facing performance dashboards
- Sales — campaign and pipeline reporting
- GTM — go-to-market reporting and analysis

## Notes
- Formerly known as Google Data Studio
- Distinct from [[Looker]] — Looker Studio is for external/client-facing reports; Looker is internal BI
- Often shared directly with hotel clients for campaign performance visibility
