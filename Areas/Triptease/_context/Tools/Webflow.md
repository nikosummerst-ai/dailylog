# Webflow

## What It Does
Website builder and CMS for marketing websites and landing pages.

## How We Use It
- Marketing team builds and manages the company website
- Landing pages and campaign pages built and published without engineering
- Direct Booking Summit (DBS) event pages hosted on Webflow

## Access
- Dashboard: https://webflow.com (Triptease workspace)

## Used By
- Marketing — company website, landing pages, event pages

## Notes
- Primary tool for triptease.com and associated marketing pages
- Direct Booking Summit event pages are a key use case
