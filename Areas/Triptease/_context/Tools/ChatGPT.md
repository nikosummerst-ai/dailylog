# ChatGPT

## What It Does
OpenAI's consumer AI assistant — web, desktop, and mobile app. Separate from the [[OpenAI API]] (developer platform). Supports GPT-5 family models, image generation, Deep Research, custom GPTs, and Projects.

## How We Use It
- General-purpose AI assistant for GTM team — drafting, research, brainstorming
- Deep Research for market/competitor analysis
- Custom GPTs for recurring workflows
- Image generation for ad-hoc marketing assets

## Access
- App: https://chatgpt.com (Google SSO available)
- Admin: manage team seats via ChatGPT Team/Enterprise workspace

## Used By
- GTM — daily-driver AI assistant alongside [[Claude API]] and [[Google Gemini]]
- Niko — general chat, research, one-off tasks where Claude isn't preferred

## Notes
- Distinct from [[OpenAI API]] — consumer/team subscription, not developer billing
- Use alongside Claude for cross-checking and when a different model perspective helps
