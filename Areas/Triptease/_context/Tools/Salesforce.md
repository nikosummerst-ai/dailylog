# Salesforce

## What It Does
Primary CRM for accounts, contacts, deals, and pipeline tracking.

## How We Use It
- Sales manages the full pipeline: prospecting, deals, and closed accounts
- CS tracks account health and renewal data
- RevOps manages data quality and reporting

## Access
- Dashboard: https://triptease.lightning.force.com

## Used By
- Sales — pipeline management, deal tracking, account and contact records
- CS — account health and renewal tracking
- RevOps — pipeline reporting, data governance, and integrations

## Notes
- Integrates with [[Clay]] for contact enrichment and import from LinkedIn
- Integrates with [[Gong]] for call activity and conversation data
- Integrates with [[Vitally]] for customer success health data
- Integrates with [[HubSpot]] for marketing data pipeline
