# Clay

## What It Does
Sales intelligence and data enrichment platform for prospecting and outreach personalisation.

## How We Use It
- Enriches contact and account data within [[Salesforce]]
- "Clip to Clay" Chrome extension imports LinkedIn contacts directly into [[Salesforce]]
- AI-generated icebreakers and personalised outreach alerts for sales reps

## Access
- Dashboard: https://www.clay.com

## Used By
- Sales — contact enrichment, LinkedIn prospecting, personalised outreach
- RevOps — data enrichment pipeline and [[Salesforce]] data quality

## Notes
- Replaced Apollo (which had replaced ZoomInfo) as the primary sales intelligence tool
- "Clip to Clay" Chrome extension is the primary LinkedIn-to-Salesforce workflow
- AI icebreakers generated automatically for new contacts
- Integrates with [[LinkedIn Sales Navigator]] for prospecting exports
