# Gong

## What It Does
Call recording, transcription, and conversation intelligence platform.

## How We Use It
- CS and Sales record and transcribe all customer calls
- Conversation intelligence surfaces coaching insights and deal risks
- Gong Dialer used for outbound calling

## Access
- Dashboard: https://us-90246.app.gong.io

## Used By
- CS — call recording for all customer conversations, post-call syncing to [[Vitally]]
- Sales — outbound calling via Gong Dialer, deal intelligence and pipeline tracking
