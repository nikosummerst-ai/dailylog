# Vitally

## What It Does
Customer success platform for managing customer health, meetings, and MRR data.

## How We Use It
- CS team manages customer health scores, meeting notes, and account status
- Sales uses for MRR and account data
- Post-call workflows sync Gong call data automatically

## Access
- Dashboard: https://triptease.vitally-eu.io

## Used By
- CS — customer health management, meeting notes, account tracking
- Sales — MRR data and account health visibility

## Notes
- Integrates with [[Gong]] for automatic call syncing after customer calls
- Integrates with [[Salesforce]] for account and contact data
- EU instance (vitally-eu.io) used for data residency compliance
