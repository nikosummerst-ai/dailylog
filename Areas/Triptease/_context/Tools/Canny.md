# Canny

## What It Does
Customer-facing product feedback and feature voting board.

## How We Use It
- Product, CS, and Sales collect and prioritise customer feature requests
- Hotel clients submit and vote on feature ideas
- Internal feedback board deprecated in favour of Future Ideas board in Canny

## Access
- Dashboard: https://triptease.canny.io

## Used By
- Product — feature prioritisation based on customer votes
- CS — collecting and surfacing client feedback
- Sales — using customer demand signals in commercial conversations

## Notes
- Internal feedback board has been deprecated; Future Ideas board now primary
- Part of the PM tools suite alongside [[Fullstory]], [[Mixpanel]], and [[Looker]]
