# OKRs and Strategic Priorities

Current company-wide focus. Update each half (H1/H2). Source: Notion "Company OKRs" page (`31e4633678978064b7dadb9e71c2d02d`).

---

## H1 2026 Theme

> **"Stop the Bleed on Churn"**

Three Key Results structure the company's work for H1 2026.

---

## KR1 — Stop the Bleed

**Target:** Keep gross MRR churn below £80k in H1 2026.

**Context:** ~£79k already churned, another £37k at risk.

**Focus areas:**
- Proactive retention
- Faster product iteration
- Core-product adoption
- Product ↔ CS ↔ Growth ↔ Engineering collaboration
- Clearer value demonstration to customers

---

## KR2 — Platform Stickiness with AI

**Target:** Deliver **40 externally-facing AI initiatives** in H1 2026.

**Framing:** *Insight → Recommendation → Action.* Every AI feature should move a customer along this chain.

**Active initiatives (examples):**
- [[Tools/Triptease Design System MCP|Design system chat prototypes]]
- M1 → M2 Messages migrator
- Date Boost Advisor
- M2 budget recommendations
- Persona insights ([[Guest Personas]])
- Guest Intelligence
- Guided campaign creation

See [[AI at Triptease]] for the full AI strategy.

---

## KR3 — Rapid Internal AI Adoption

**Target:** Deepen AI use across every function.

**Current state:**
- **81%** of employees actively using AI
- **3x** token consumption growth since start of 2026
- **9 people** on Claude Max tier

**Behaviours to drive:**

## Strategic Bets

### Agent-First Product
Charlie Osmond's thesis (Mar 2026, `#ai-chat`): Feb/Mar 2026 was the inflexion point for "spinning up agent teams." Belief: every Tripteaser's job is moving toward managing agents. Formal strategic workstream "Track B" scoped at the Agent-First Workshop (15 April 2026) with Product/Eng leads + SMT.

### Mid-Market Focus (ICP)
Triptease has deliberately stepped away from Enterprise (Marriott/IHG) — too bespoke. Focus is mid-market hotels with **LTV:CAC > 10x**.

### Two Pricing Models
- **License (fixed)**
- **CPC / Pay-Per-Stay (performance)**

PPX commission model matters for billing automations — see [[Org Structure#Commercial Data|Commercial Data]] squad.
