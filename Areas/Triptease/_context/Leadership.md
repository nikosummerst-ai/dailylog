Senior team and named leaders at Triptease. See [[Org Structure]] for how teams are arranged and [[About Triptease]] for the company overview.

## Founders / C-Suite

### James Osmond — CEO
Charlie's brother. CEO of Triptease. Public-facing on partnerships and industry events.

### Charlie Osmond — Co-founder & "Chief Tease" / CGO
Co-founded Triptease in 2012. Oxford Engineering grad; previous business Fresh Minds; HSMAI "Extraordinary Mind"; won Sunday Times £5M London Entrepreneur Challenge; member of YPO Manhattan and BA Business Advisory Board. Drives the AI-first / "agent teams" thesis, customer/partner relationships, and growth.

### Alasdair Snow — Co-founder & CPO
Co-founded with Charlie in 2012. Owns product strategy and the prod-eng operating system (`#squad-stone-peng`). Deeply involved in long-range product vision.

### Alexandra Zubko — Co-founder
Joined 2014. Former Global Head of Strategy at IHG. Brought hospitality domain expertise.

### Dan Bodart — CTO / Head of Engineering
Owns AI tooling rollout (Claude with no usage limits internally), platform reliability, engineering org. Confirmed unrestricted Claude use company-wide in `#global` (April 2026).
