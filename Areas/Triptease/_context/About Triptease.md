# About Triptease

Triptease is the **pioneer of the Direct Booking Movement** — a hotel technology company that helps hotels fight back against the power of OTAs (Online Travel Agencies) with a full-funnel marketing platform that increases direct bookings and attracts more valuable guests.

> **Aspirational mission:** *"Drive OTA volumes at half the price."*

Founded in 2012, Triptease now serves **13,000+ hotel properties** across every major region. See [[Product Reference]] for the full company overview, products, integrations and competitive landscape.

---

## Quick Facts

- **Founded:** 2012, London
- **CEO:** James Osmond
- **Co-founders:** Charlie Osmond ("Chief Tease" / CGO), Alasdair Snow (CPO), Alexandra Zubko (joined 2014)
- **Funding:** ~$24M raised across 8 rounds. Investors include Notion Capital, Episode 1, Business Growth Fund, BOOST&Co
- **Customers:** 13,000+ hotels (15,000+ on platform), from 12-room independents to global chains
- **Offices:** London (HQ — 75 Bermondsey St), Barcelona, New York, Singapore
- **Awards:** Hotel Tech Awards Best Direct Booking Tool (2020, 2021, 2022); Sunday Times Tech Track 100 (#27, 2020)
- **Employees:** "Tripteasers" — distributed across UK, Europe, US, APAC

See [[Leadership]] for the wider exec and senior team.

## How We're Structured

Engineering runs on a **squad model** (Spotify-style pods with PM + designer + engineers), grouped under product groups. Non-engineering functions: Customer Success, Sales (Global, Americas, EMEA, APAC, SMB), Marketing (Demand Gen, Events, Growth, Rev Ops), People & Ops, Finance, Partnerships.

## Strategic Priorities (H1 2026)

The current company theme is **"Stop the Bleed on Churn"** with three objectives:

1. **Stop the Bleed** — keep gross MRR churn below £80k in H1 2026
2. **Platform Stickiness with AI** — deliver 40 externally-facing AI initiatives (Insight → Recommendation → Action)
3. **Rapid Internal AI Adoption** — 81% of employees actively using AI; 3x token consumption growth in 2026
