# About Triptease

Triptease is a hotel technology company that helps hotels drive more direct bookings and reduce dependency on OTAs (Online Travel Agencies).

## Products
- **Parity** — rate parity monitoring across OTAs
- **Retargeting** — targeted ads to bring guests back to direct booking
- **Spotlight** — hotel search and discovery
- **Price Feed** — competitive pricing data
