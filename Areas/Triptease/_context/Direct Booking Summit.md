# Direct Booking Summit (DBS)

Triptease's **flagship owned industry event**. Brings together hotel marketing, revenue, and commercial leaders to share best practice on direct booking, AI, and hotel marketing strategy.

---

## Format

- **Multi-city annually** — runs in NORAM, EMEA, APAC editions
- **Audience:** hotel marketing/revenue/commercial leaders, partners, agencies
- **Content:** keynotes, panels, case studies, hands-on workshops
- **Owned end-to-end** by Triptease — not a sponsorship slot at someone else's conference
