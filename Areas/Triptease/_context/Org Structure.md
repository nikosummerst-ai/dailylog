## Engineering Squads

Engineering runs on a **squad model** — Spotify-style pods (PM + designer + engineers) grouped under product groups. Squads own products end-to-end.

#### Dragonfly
**Owns:** Messages, Email, Onsite Price Match, Guest Behavioural Data
**Slack:** `#squad-dragonfly`
