# DBS - Guest Personas

## Key Decisions
- Slim format: front cover + 3 personas + back cover with QR code
- 3-persona selection criteria:
  1. High converter with massive revenue impact
  2. High converter in niche category
  3. High converter with high volume
- Scrapped BigQuery client data to make process scalable
- Decided against manual validation by CS/sales reps (67 attendees, ~40 unique properties too many)
- Navigate retrospective: last-minute requests and poor preparation caused issues; US printing uses letter size not A4
- [[Cloudprinter]] approach preferred (API integration, auto-finds nearest fulfillment center)
