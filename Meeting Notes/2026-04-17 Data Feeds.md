## Problem Discovery
- Testing with Revinate CRM (Boardwalk) revealed no shared identifier between BigQuery guest journey data and CRM data
- Booking IDs generated by Triptease don't get stored in the CRM — CRMs only store booking engine references
- Check-in/check-out date matching unreliable for smaller hotels with overlapping bookings
- Current BigQuery exports are anonymized with no email data
- CRM data captures minimal info — mostly just people and stay duration, no ancillary data
