## Discussion

### Validation Process and Technical Approach
[[Niko Summers]] will batch all hotels and prepare reports to the persona selection stage. Reps access a unique dashboard link to select and verify personas for each report. The dashboard uses the Events tab with limited access; when reps generate/select segments, report links automatically feed into a spreadsheet. For prospects without account owners, the marketing team handles validation by checking reviews and the hotel website. Reps will not see account owner assignments due to the batch approach — they pick reports manually.
