## Discussion

### Validation Process & Technical Approach
[[Niko Summers]] will batch all hotels and prepare reports up to persona selection stage. Reps access a unique dashboard link to select and verify [[Guest Personas]]; the dashboard uses the Events tab with limited access. When reps generate/select segments, report links automatically feed into a spreadsheet. For prospects without account owners, the marketing team can validate by checking reviews and the hotel website. Reps won't see account owner assignments due to the batch approach — they pick reports manually.
