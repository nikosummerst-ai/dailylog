# Agent-First Workshop — Outcomes & Commitments

## Attendees
- Product & Engineering leadership (full attendee list in source transcript)

## Key Decisions
- Triptease adopts an **agent-first** strategic stance: order-of-magnitude productivity amplifier for hotel marketing teams — not human replacement, not "AI features bolted onto M2"
- **MCPs are table stakes**: every feature (new or evolved) must expose a clean MCP/skill contract
- Two parallel tracks: Track A (distributed, all squads, immediate) and Track B (AI-native incubator, pending SMT decision)
- Scope expands beyond Triptease-operated channels — agents should reason and act across OTAs and the wider marketing stack
- **[[DBS Event]] Mexico City** (4 weeks at time of writing) is the first external proof point, framed as "decide faster, act faster, learn faster"

## Discussion

### Why Now
SaaStr data: ~30% of increased marketing spend shifting to AI tooling; traditional spend down ~35%. Customers already working around Triptease limitations using Claude and similar tools. Competitors (Cody, Revinate) shipping MCP and agent capabilities. Triptease currently influences ~0.5% of hotel revenue; completing M2 as planned does not change the shape of the business.

### Agent Portfolio
**Productivity inside today's scope:** Performance reporting, Creative + Branding (paired), Bulk edits, Budget Advisor v2.
**Scope-expanding bets:** Campaign Strategy & Planning, OTA/third-party channel strategy, [[Guest Personas]] (productised from Niko's prototype), Marketing Health Monitor.
**Deprioritised:** OTA parity insight advisor; comp-set pricing as a near-term bet.

### Track B — AI-Native Incubator
Hypothesis: the genuinely disruptive version of Triptease is not the current platform with AI bolted on. A small dedicated squad to explore a Claude co-worker style interaction paradigm. [[Alasdair Snow]] and Dan to scope staffing and explicit kill/scale decision points for SMT review.

### DBS Demo Plan
- Day 1 (20 min): Triptease Agents principles — built-in expertise, capable of action, proactive, user in control
- Day 2 (20 min, Chimmy): Live demos — performance reporting agent (real MCP + real hotel data), budget planning agent, [[Guest Personas]] agent, creative + branding (demo-ware acceptable)
- CAB Monday: hands-on session with hoteliers on trust and autonomy thresholds
